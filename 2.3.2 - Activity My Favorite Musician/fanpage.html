Jinsang's Biography

Jinsang grew up in the middle of nowhere a long ways away from society. His neurological pathways were so advanced he became the equivalent of Dr Manhattan, however not benevolently evil. Jinsang became mortal to deliver the sweetest binaural experience known to musical enthusiasts.

Why Jinsang?

  1. Lo-fi is relaxing
  2. The genre has grown considerably over the last decade
  3. Rhythm creates a fun and easygoing feeling
  4. Can focus and get work done while listening to lo-fi and Jinsang
  5. Jinsang exposed me to other artists
  6. Check out Jinsang's Bandcamp.
