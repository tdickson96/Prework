Jinsang's Biography

Jinsang grew up in the middle of nowhere a long ways away from society. His neurological pathways were so advanced he became the equivalent of Dr Manhattan, however not benevolently evil. Anyways, Jinsang became a mortal to deliver the sweetest binaural experience known to musical afficionados.

Why Jinsang?

  • Lo-fi is relaxing
  • The genre has grown considerably over the last decade
  • Rhythm creates a fun and easygoing feeling
  • Can focus and get work done while listening to lo-fi and Jinsang
  • Jinsang exposed me to other artists
  • Jinsang's Bandcamp profile led me to discover featured artists
